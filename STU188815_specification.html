Ardened Body & Mind Mini Site Specification

Objective/Goals:

The objective of the Ardened Body & Mind mini site is to promote physical activity while also engaging visitors in intellectual and mindful practices. The site aims to provide resources, activities, and information that encourage users to explore various forms of movement and incorporate them into their daily lives. Additionally, it seeks to foster a sense of mindfulness and self-awareness, encouraging users to connect mind and body through movement.

Key Audience(s):

  1. Emma, the Busy Professional:

    Emma is a 32-year-old marketing executive with a hectic schedule. She struggles to find time for physical activity and often feels stressed and overwhelmed. Goals: Emma seeks convenient ways to incorporate movement into her busy lifestyle and desires activities that help her relax and unwind after long workdays.

  2. Alex, the Fitness Enthusiast:

    Alex is a 25-year-old fitness trainer who is passionate about staying active and healthy. He enjoys exploring new forms of exercise and is always looking for ways to challenge himself physically and mentally. Goals: Alex is interested in discovering innovative movement practices that enhance his fitness routine and improve his overall well-being.

Provisional Site Structure:

  1. Home Page
  2. About Us
  3. Movement Practices
  4. Mindfulness Resources
  5. Community Forum
  6. Contact Us

Functional Specification:

  • User Registration/Login
  • Search Functionality
  • Responsive Design
  • Interactive Elements
  • Feedback Mechanism
  • Integration with Social Media

Competitor Analysis:

  1. Yoga International: Offers a comprehensive library of yoga classes and mindfulness practices, but lacks diversity in movement options.
  2. Headspace: Primarily focused on meditation and mindfulness, with limited emphasis on physical movement.
  3. Daily Burn: Provides a wide range of fitness classes, including yoga and pilates, but lacks specific mindfulness resources.

Differentiation Strategy: Ardened Body & Mind aims to differentiate itself by offering a unique blend of physical activity and mindfulness practices, catering to individuals seeking holistic well-being. By providing diverse movement options and comprehensive mindfulness resources, the site aims to appeal to a wide audience interested in enhancing both their physical and mental health. Additionally, the community forum fosters a sense of belonging and support, distinguishing Mindful Movement as not just a website but a supportive community for personal growth and exploration.
